Who We Are

A Kenyan School with a Global Outlook

NewDawn School is a dynamic learning community offering holistic education from Kindergarten to Year 12. Located in the heart of Bondo, Siaya County, we nurture each learner academically, socially, emotionally, and spiritually.

We embrace Kenya‚Äôs Competency Based Curriculum (CBC) framework while exposing learners to international standards, digital literacy and strong moral values. Instruction is primarily in English, with Kiswahili used actively in learning and daily school life.

Inclusion, Safeguarding & Quality

Every learner matters. We are intentional about safety, dignity and support for diverse needs.

Inclusive Education & Disability Support

  • We welcome learners with mild to moderate learning differences and collaborate with parents on support plans.
  • Classrooms prioritise clear signage, visual schedules and differentiated instruction.
  • Reasonable accommodations are considered based on individual needs.
  • Teachers receive ongoing training on inclusive, learner-friendly practices.

Child Protection & Safeguarding

  • Zero tolerance for bullying, harassment or abuse ‚Äì reporting channels are communicated to learners and parents.

Quality Standards & Governance
